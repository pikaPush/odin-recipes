Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish that has become a global favorite, known for its irresistible combination of flavors and textures. It typically features a thin, crispy or soft crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and a variety of toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, or fresh basil. Baked in a hot oven until the cheese is bubbly and golden, pizza offers a perfect balance of savory, cheesy, and slightly tangy taste. Whether enjoyed as a quick snack, a casual meal, or a party favorite, pizza brings people together with its comforting and customizable appeal.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (around 110°F or 43°C)
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Make the Sauce
  3. Preheat and Shape the Dough
  4. Assemble the Pizza
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes
